1. Containers & Padding

Bootstrap uses .container, .container-fluid, and .container-{breakpoint} for responsive widths.

.container — Fixed-width, centered, responsive.
.container-fluid — Full width at all breakpoints.

Padding Utilities

p-1 (0.25rem) p-2 (0.5rem) p-3 (1rem) p-4 (1.5rem) 
<div class="container">...fixed width...</div>
<div class="container-fluid">...full width...</div>
<div class="p-3">padding 1rem all sides</div>
<div class="px-5">padding left & right 3rem</div>
<div class="py-4">padding top & bottom 1.5rem</div>
